FRIED RICE


About:

Fried rice is a rice-based comfort meal consisting of rice (preferrably a day old) toppings that is pan fried at a high heat! It's a super quick and easy dish to make, but it's also super delicious.


Ingredients:
  • Rice (DAY OLD)
  • Protein
  • Seasonings (e.g. Soy Sauce, Chili Oil)
  • Toppings (egg and green onions always slap)

How to make:
  1. Add oil to a pan or wok and set the stove to high heat
  2. Add the rice and seasonings and cook till its light brown
  3. Add your toppings and protein and cook until it's cooked
  4. Place the cooked fried rice into a bowl
  5. Enjoy!
