MAZEMEN


About:

Mazemen is a brothless noodle dish that features similar trimmings to its "brothful" counterpart! With it's emphasis on it's topping and seasonings, the dish adds a delightful twist to its noodle family.


Ingredients:
  • Noodles
  • Protein (Typically ground pork)
  • Seasonings (e.g. Soy Sauce, Chili Oil)
  • Toppings

How to make:
  1. Cook the noodles to desired firmness
  2. Prepare desired toppings
  3. Add the cooked noodles to the bowl and mix in seasonings
  4. Finish with the prepared toppings
  5. Enjoy!
