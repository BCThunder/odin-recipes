Poke


About:

Poke is a raw seasoned fish dish, typically served with rice, and garnished with toppings, creating a refreshing and delicious meal from the ocean!


Ingredients:
  • Raw Fish of Choice (e.g. Salmon, Tuna)
  • Base (Rice, Lettuce, Chips)
  • Sauce (e.g. Soy Sauce, Sriracha)
  • Toppings

How to make:
  1. Chop the fish into cubes
  2. Prepare your base, toppings, and sauce
  3. Mix the ingredients and put them in a bowl
  4. Enjoy!
